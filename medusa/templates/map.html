FEDERAL PROTECTIONS
Violence Against Women Act (VAWA)
Federal law — tools and grants for DV, sexual assault, stalking response.
Title IX (1972)
Prohibits sex discrimination in federally funded education. Schools must respond to sexual assault.
Title VII — Civil Rights Act
Prohibits workplace sexual harassment and sex discrimination.
18 U.S.C. § 2261 — Interstate DV
Federal crime to cross state lines to commit domestic violence or violate a protective order.
Dobbs v. Jackson (2022)
Eliminated federal abortion rights. Active litigation in 20+ states. Disproportionate impact on rape and incest survivors.
Women's Bureau — Proposed Closure 2025
Trump budget proposed eliminating the 105-year-old Women's Bureau, which administered VAWA workplace grants covering 50M+ workers.

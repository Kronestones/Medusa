LAWS THAT PROTECTED WOMEN
1973 — Overturned 2022
Roe v. Wade. Federal constitutional right to abortion. Protected for 49 years. Overturned by Dobbs v. Jackson in a 6-3 decision shaped by three Trump appointees. As of 2025, 21 states have banned or severely restricted abortion — including for rape and incest survivors.
1994 — Active
Violence Against Women Act (VAWA). Funds DV shelters, rape crisis centers, and legal aid. Reauthorized through 2027. 2025 Trump budget proposed gutting its grant programs.
1972 — Weakened 2025
Title IX. Prohibits sex discrimination in federally funded education. Requires schools to respond to sexual assault. Trump administration rolled back protections again in 2025.
1964 — Active
Title VII — Civil Rights Act. Prohibits workplace sexual harassment and sex discrimination.
1994 — Active
18 U.S.C. § 2261 — Interstate DV. Federal crime to cross state lines to commit domestic violence or violate a protective order.
LAWS THAT HAVE HURT WOMEN
Never Passed
Equal Rights Amendment. "Equality of rights shall not be denied on account of sex." Passed Congress 1972. Never ratified. Republican legislatures have blocked it for over 50 years. Without the ERA, sex discrimination has no explicit constitutional prohibition.
Legal in 37 states
Child Marriage. Only 13 states have a complete ban. Girls can be married with parental consent, sometimes as young as 12. Frequently used to legitimize statutory rape and prevents victims from leaving without parental consent.
1873 — Being Revived
Comstock Act. Victorian law being revived to ban mailing abortion medication across state lines — which would end medication abortion nationally even where it is legal.
Until 1993
Marital Rape. Legal in all 50 states until North Carolina criminalized it July 5, 1993. Some states still require additional proof of force to prosecute spousal rape.
2025
SAVE Act. Voter ID requirements disproportionately impact women with name changes from marriage. An estimated 69 million women lack required documentation.
2025
Women's Bureau — Proposed Elimination. Trump proposed closing the 105-year-old DOL Women's Bureau. DOGE canceled Women in Apprenticeship grants, calling them "wasteful DEI."
